‚ö†Ô∏è EXAM TRAP: NEITHER extreme position is correct. Don't defend pure nativism OR pure empiricism. The answer is ALWAYS interaction: innate capacity + environmental input. Poverty of Stimulus shows syntax is innate, but actual language requires environmental exposure (no input = no language, as neglect cases show).

‚ö° KEY EXAM MOVE: The False Dichotomy

The question "Is X innate or learned?" is BADLY FRAMED. Correct answer: "The capacity may be innate (genetic substrate), but expression requires environmental input. P = G + E means both factors operate jointly." Use spoken vs written language as demonstration: spoken grammar emerges naturally (nativist component) but reading requires intensive instruction (empiricist component).

LO2-A Measuring Nature: SNP Association Studies
"Describe the methodology for measuring genetic contributions using SNP association studies..."
üß¨ SNP Association Study Methodology
1. SNP Definition: Single Nucleotide Polymorphism ("snip") ‚Äî one DNA location with 2 possible amino acid variants. Notation: Common = A (big), Rare = a (small). Genotypes: AA Aa aa
2. Recruitment: Two groups: Cases (with trait/disorder) vs Controls (without). Matched on age, SES, education.
3. DNA Extraction: Cheek swab, saliva, or blood sample. Genome purified and placed on microarray chips.
4. Analysis: Automated scanning for SNP frequencies at specific genomic locations. Statistical comparison between groups.
5. Interpretation: If variant (e.g., aa) significantly more frequent in cases ‚Üí "associated" with trait. BUT: Association ‚â† Causation!
‚ö†Ô∏è METHODOLOGICAL LIMITATION: Associated variants may not directly CAUSE the trait. They may just be "tagging along" with actual causal variants (linkage disequilibrium). This is a major problem for GWAS ("fishing trip" approach) ‚Äî can identify regions coding for seemingly unrelated proteins.

‚ö° EXAM TIP: Hormonal Conditions Are ENVIRONMENT

Students often misclassify prenatal hormonal exposure as "nature." In utero hormonal environment (e.g., testosterone levels affecting brain development) counts as ENVIRONMENTAL factor ‚Äî it's the physical-chemical environment the foetus develops in, not the foetus's own genes.
LO1/LO4 The 5 Myths & Misconceptions ‚Äî Critical Evaluation
"Critically evaluate common misconceptions about the nature-nurture debate..."
MYTH 1: Genes Dictate Outcomes

Eye colour ‚Üí ok. Favourite food ‚Üí ok. Intelligence ‚Üí NO!

‚úì REALITY: Genes set "limiting factors" (reaction range), not deterministic outcomes. Mini-myth: Biological differences must be genetic ‚Äî WRONG. Environment changes biology (poverty reduces hippocampal volume). Biology influences environment (shy temperament ‚Üí avoids social situations ‚Üí reinforces shyness).

MYTH 2: Tabula Rasa ‚Äî "Society Is Failing"

Blaming crime, poverty, racism on genes OR environment exclusively.

‚úì REALITY: Human bias ‚Üí negative attributes "not my fault" / positive attributes "all because of me." Genes make convenient scapegoat. ALL phenotypes (positive AND negative) influenced by BOTH G + E. Parent preferring ADHD diagnosis over ODD because ADHD feels "more biological" (both ~40% heritable!).

MYTH 3: Warrior Gene (MAOA) Determines Violence

MAOA-L used to reduce prison sentences. High proportion of offenders have it.

‚úì REALITY: 95% of MAOA-L carriers are NOT violent. Majority ‚â† majority. Nearly half of violent offenders have MAOA-H. Gene neither NECESSARY nor SUFFICIENT. Poor individual prediction (PPV ‚âà 5%). G√óE interaction required (maltreatment context).

MYTH 4: "All Traits Heritable So Heritability Is Irrelevant"

Academic "navel gazing" with no clinical application.

‚úì REALITY: Heritability MAGNITUDE varies (10%-81%) ‚Äî clinically meaningful! Same surface behaviour, different mechanisms:
Conduct Disorder: Low CU = 30% h¬≤ (responds to parenting intervention) | High CU = 81% h¬≤ (resists environmental intervention, needs biological targets)

MYTH 5: Public Will Misinterpret Genetics ‚Üí Fatalism

"Fat genes" reduce locus of control. ADHD boy: "my brain is different, can't improve."

‚úì REALITY: Problem is COMMUNICATION, not genetic information itself. "70% genetic" misinterpreted as "70% unchangeable." Solution = education (including psychologists) pairing genetic risk with intervention efficacy to PRESERVE agency. What can psychology do? Early intervention optimizes gene expression!

‚ö° UNIFIED TAKEAWAY FOR ALL 5 MYTHS

P = G + E is not just a formula ‚Äî it's a worldview. Every myth fails because it treats G and E as COMPETING explanations rather than INTERACTING factors. High heritability identifies intervention targets, not intervention impossibility. Genetic findings should ENHANCE psychological treatment design by revealing mechanisms, not undermine it by fostering determinism.
