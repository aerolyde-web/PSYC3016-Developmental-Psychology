Q15
Control of Variables (COV) strategy is:
A) Innate from birth
B) A domain-general abstract strategy (change one variable at a time) that must be explicitly taught
C) Impossible for children to learn
D) Only useful in labs
üí° Explanation:
COV = systematic experimental design principle:
  • Hold all variables constant except ONE
  • Test that variable's effect
  • Repeat for each variable
  • Allows isolating causal effects
Domain-general: Applies to ANY experimental situation (physics, biology, social experiments). Children don't spontaneously discover it‚Äîneeds explicit teaching. But once taught, can apply across domains.
