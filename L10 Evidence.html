Intervention Type Target Mechanism Effectiveness Key Principle Why It Works/Fails
Reward-Based Systems Cold variant Leverage intact reward processing Moderate success if rewards large enough "Bigger carrots, not bigger sticks" Works with biology, not against it
Trauma-Focused CBT Hot variant Address underlying trauma Good response in secondary CU "Heal the wound, reduce the rage" Targets root cause of emotional dysregulation

Traditional Punishment Behavior control Fear/consequence learning Ineffective "Can't teach what they can't feel" Impaired fear conditioning prevents learning
Stimulation Activities Under-arousal Provide prosocial stimulation Reduces antisocial seeking "Channel the need for excitement" Meets biological need legally
